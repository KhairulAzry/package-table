Employee Self Service

Employee is able to view and edit personal or tax information, view/download HR documents and tax forms, check leave balance and apply for leave, manage claims, participate in performance appraisal and enroll for courses of the client’s skill development programs.
HR Services

HR is able to view and manage employees’ information, set system access authority, employee on-boarding / off-boarding process management, e-leave / e-claim application management and download, view or configure information for all employees.
HR Business Intelligence Genius (hr-BIG)

A powerful analytics and reporting tool that converts real-time data into charts for strategic decision making leading to better business outcomes.
Performance Appraisal

Computerized performance appraisal to evaluate, manage, compare, and consolidate performance based on user-defined key performance indicator (KPI) criteria with performance reports generation.
Succession Planning

Prepare employee for the next key role with job analysis recording through user-defined competencies profile used to assess the criteria for future orientation.
Skills Development

A learning content management system that analyses learning initiatives, design and develop learning content strategies in order to empower employees of the desired areas of growth.
Workforce Planning

Systematic process of identifying and addressing the gaps between the current workforce status and the future workforce needs.
Digital Hiring

Integrated recruitment management suite that automates the processes such as successive candidate information processing, reports generation and other HR functions.
Digital Document

A convenient way to interactively create and electronically circulate documents. This also includes integrated documents, e-signature and reports.
Enterprose Payroll

Process payroll for all employees by configuring amounts for allowances, basic salary, commission, deduction, overtime pay, personal income tax and etc. with statutory compliance automation for ASEAN.
360° Feedback

Feedback platform from both client’s internal parties as well as external parties (customers, vendors & partners) with various set templates of feedback forms from which feedback can be given. The forms can be set based on the reviewer’s profile.
Report

The system is able to generate report summaries of; payroll, allowance, flexi benefit, deduction, leave, leave process, turnover, flexi benefit process, statutory forms for ASEAN, employee master profile, training, qualification, disciplinary, career path, year-to-date (YTD) payroll report, payslip generation and distribution.
